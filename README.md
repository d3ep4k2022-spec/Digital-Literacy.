Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study and Prevention Guide
Summary: I researched a prevalent cybercrime scenario and created a safety checklist specifically for the Indian student community.

Folder Link: task-5-cybercrime/

Project Report: I conducted a case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud, specifically the "QR Code Scam."

The Insight: Most cybercrimes today rely on social engineering—creating urgency and confusion—rather than sophisticated technical hacking. Victims are often tricked into entering their UPI PIN under the false premise of "receiving" money.

Personal Reflection: What surprised me most was how easily human behavior can be exploited during a transaction. Many people believe they are safe because they haven't shared a password, not realizing that a PIN entry is a direct authorization of a "push" transaction.

Habit Change: I will now strictly follow the "No PIN for Receiving" rule and have implemented a monthly App Permission Audit on my devices to ensure no unnecessary background data tracking is occurring.

Do's

-Do keep your privacy settings updated to control who sees your personal information.

-Do use professional profile pictures on platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub.

-Do verify information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation or "fake news".

-Do network with industry professionals and alumni in your field of study.

-Do use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
 


Don'ts

-Don't post offensive or controversial content that could damage your professional reputation.

-Don't share sensitive personal data like your home address or bank details publicly.

-Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying or online harassment.

-Don't plagiarize or share copyrighted material without proper attribution.

-Don't click on suspicious links or download unverified attachments.
